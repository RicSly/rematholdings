About Us

Remat Holdings Limited is a diversified company with an exceptional reputation for ethical, progressive, sustainable and global business best practices, headquartered in Accra, Ghana with operations across Africa.

Our Vision

Building Africa's Sustainable Future through transformative solutions that address local challenges while meeting international standards.

Our Mission

To leverage technology and innovation to transform diverse sectors of the African economy including finance, real estate, agriculture, and mining.

Geographic Footprint

Headquartered in Accra, Ghana with a growing operational footprint across selected African markets through partnerships and project deployments.

Our Services

We operate through a multi-vertical holding structure, combining direct investments, subsidiary operations, strategic partnerships and advisory services.
