Our Recruitment Process

A systematic approach to finding and placing the right talent for your organization

1

Needs Assessment & Planning

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your organizational needs, culture, and specific role requirements. This includes job analysis, competency mapping, and recruitment strategy development.

2

Sourcing & Candidate Identification

Utilizing our extensive database, professional networks, and targeted search methods to identify qualified candidates. We employ both active and passive candidate sourcing strategies across Africa.

3

Screening & Assessment

Rigorous screening including competency-based interviews, technical assessments, reference checks, and background verification to ensure candidate suitability and cultural fit.

4

Selection & Offer Management

Facilitating the final selection process, negotiating employment terms, and managing the offer process to ensure successful candidate onboarding and satisfaction.

5

Onboarding & Post-Placement Support

Comprehensive onboarding support and regular follow-up to ensure successful integration. We provide post-placement check-ins and performance monitoring for up to 12 months.
