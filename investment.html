Our Investment Process

A disciplined approach to identifying, evaluating, and managing investments

1

Sourcing & Deal Origination

Active sourcing through our extensive network, strategic partnerships, and proprietary deal flow channels across Africa. We maintain relationships with entrepreneurs, industry experts, and co-investors.

2

Screening & Due Diligence

Rigorous evaluation including market analysis, financial modeling, management assessment, and technical due diligence. We assess strategic fit, growth potential, and risk factors.

3

Structuring & Negotiation

Developing optimal investment structures, negotiating terms, and preparing legal documentation. We focus on alignment of interests, governance, and exit planning from day one.

4

Portfolio Management

Active involvement through board participation, strategic guidance, operational support, and performance monitoring. We work closely with management teams to drive growth and value creation.

5

Exit Planning & Execution

Strategic planning for optimal exit timing and pathways including trade sales, secondary sales, or public listings. We maximize returns while ensuring business continuity.

Investment Criteria

Key factors we evaluate when considering investment opportunities

Market Potential

Minimum $100M addressable market with strong growth drivers and favorable competitive dynamics.

Management Team

Experienced, committed leadership with relevant industry expertise and execution capability.

Innovation Edge

Differentiated products, services, or business models with sustainable competitive advantages.

Strategic Fit

Alignment with our sector focus, geographic priorities, and value creation capabilities.

Financial Metrics

Clear path to profitability, attractive unit economics, and potential for 3-5x return on investment.

Impact Potential

Positive social, environmental, or economic impact alongside financial returns.
